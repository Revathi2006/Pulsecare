PulseCare Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025

At PulseCare, your privacy and data security are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect:
  • Personal information (like name, email, contact details) for appointment management.
  • Health-related data (such as appointment details, reminders) to improve your healthcare experience.
  • Usage data (like interaction logs) to help us make PulseCare better.
How We Use Your Information:
  • To schedule appointments and manage hospital queues.
  • To send reminders for appointments, medicines, and water intake.
  • To improve features like smart scheduling and chatbot support.
  • To ensure a smooth and personalized experience inside the app.
Data Protection:

We store your data securely using trusted technologies like Firebase. Your information will never be sold or shared with third parties without your consent.

Your Control:

You can request access, update, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting our support team.
